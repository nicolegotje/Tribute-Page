Matthew Mercer

Dungeon Master that is a nerdy ass voice actor!

Timeline of Matthew Mercer's work:

  • 1982 - Matthew Mercer was born as Matthew Christopher Miller in Palm Beach Gardes, Florida.
  • 2004 to 2013 - He did work as a voice actor for many major video games/anime cartoons. Some of the major games/shows were Naruto, Sailor Moon, Fairy Tail, Dragon Mall Super, JoJo's Bizarre Adventures, DC Super Friends, Beware the Batman, Marvel's Spider Man, Resident Evil, Justice League War, Mortal Kombat Legends, World of Warcraft, Final Fantasy Fables, Super Street Fighter IV, Guild Wars 2, Fallout, Xenoblade Chronicles, Lego DC, and much more.
  • 2015 to 2017 - Matthew Mercer was the DM for Critical Role, Campaign One, Vox Machina.
  • 2018 to 2021 He DM'ed for Critical Role, Campaign Two, Mighty Nein.
  • 2021 and 2022 - Matthew played as Dariax Zaveon, a dwarf socercer, in Exandria Unlimited.
  • 2021 to present - Matthew is now DMing Critical Role, Campain Three, Hell's Bells.
  • 2022 - He voiced over many of his character from Campain One in the new Animated Series of Vox Machina that is aired on Amazon Prime.
